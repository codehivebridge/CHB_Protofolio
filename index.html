How free downloads work

  1. Open a free project and tap "Get free download."
  2. Subscribe to the YouTube channel using the button in the popup.
  3. Enter your email address and tap "Request download" — this opens your email app with everything already filled in.
  4. Get your source code — it's sent to that email once your subscription is confirmed, usually within a day.

Subscriptions are checked by hand, not automatically — so make sure the email you enter is one you actually check.

How paid downloads work

  1. Open a paid project and tap "Buy via UPI." A QR code and UPI ID appear, already filled in with that project's price.
  2. Pay with any UPI app — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank's app.
  3. Send the payment screenshot along with the project name, using the WhatsApp or email button in the same window.
  4. Get your source code — it's sent to you directly once the payment is confirmed, usually within a day.

Payments are verified by hand, not by an automatic gateway — so keep the screenshot until you've received your files.
